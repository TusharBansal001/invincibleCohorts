ASSAM

Stretching along the length of the mighty Brahmaputra River, Assam features green valleys and blue hills, serving as a gateway to northeast Indian states. An Assam itinerary can lead from important Hindu pilgrimage sites and Buddhist temples to an elephant safari in the World Heritage-listed national park. With its thriving rural life, the state offers a number of picturesque ethnic villages, as well as world-famous tea plantations. Whether it's hiking around South Asia's largest freshwater island or exploring India's only coal museum, a trip to Assam will satisfy a wide range of interests. Assam is in India.

Elephant And Jeep Safari At Kaziranga National Park

rip Starting From: Guwahati Trip Location: Guwahati About the Activity: Sprawling over an area of 430 sq km, Kaziranga National Park is the only natural home to the imposing one-horned Rhinos in the world. Travel to the deep jungles with this 3 day package. Located between the suburbs of Golaghat and Nagaon districts, Assam, the park also has a significant number of Elephants, Indian Tigers and Asiatic Buffalos. Indulge in Elephant Safari and Jeep Safari which are the best ways to explore and unfold the vigor and splendor of this sanctuary. The package includes food, accommodation and transportation which is inclusive of pick up/ drop.
